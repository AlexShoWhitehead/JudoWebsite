Cornell Judo Club

This is the Cornell Judo Clubs official website. The Cornell Judo Club is one of the countrys oldest clubs, dating back over 40 years. We are located in Friedman Wrestling Center on the Cornell University campus in Ithaca, NY. We are the only judo school in the Ithaca area.

Members of the public and Cornell community are welcome. We do allow children to participate, but because we do not have a separate class, we ask that parents come along to supervise and/or participate along with their children.

We will ask you to sign a liability waiver and join USA Judo, which provides us (and you) with additional insurance coverage. If you have additional questions, contact Alexander Whitehead, a club officer at asw236 (at) cornell (dot) edu.

Classes Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays from 7:30-9:30pm
