We welcome anyone of any skill level! We are always excited to meet new people and to teach!

At around 50 years old, we are one of the longest running judo clubs on the East Coast!

There is free parking after 5pm, or you can take any TCat headed towards the veterinary school, walk, or bike!

We work closely with the BJJ and Muay Thai clubs. If you come for judo, you are free to practice any of the three sports as we all practice together in the same building at the same time!
